Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus

Family: Felidae

Weight: 34 - 64 kg

Height: 67 - 94 cm

Facts: The cheetah's unique body structure: flexible spine, semi-retractable claws, long legs and tail allow it to achieve the unbelievable top speed of 110 km/hr (70 mph). The cheetah's body is narrow and lightweight with long slender limbs. Specialized muscles allow for a greater swing to the limbs increasing acceleration.
